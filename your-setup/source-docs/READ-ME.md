# Drop your existing documents here

If you already have material that describes your brand, voice, audience, or product, put
it in this folder before you run `/setup`. The setup will read it and fill in as much as
it can automatically, so you answer fewer questions.

## What's useful here

- Brand or style guide
- Tone of voice document
- ICP, buyer persona, or audience research
- Product one-pager, sales page, or feature list
- Past content briefs or messaging docs
- An exported About page or positioning statement

Formats: `.md`, `.txt`, `.pdf`, `.docx`, or just paste links during `/setup` and Claude
will fetch them.

## What does NOT go here

- Your best example **articles** go in `../voice-examples/` instead. Those teach the
  engine your writing style; the docs here teach it your facts and rules.

> Files you add here stay on your computer. They are ignored by git so you do not
> accidentally share them when you push the repo.
